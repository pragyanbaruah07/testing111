Assam

Assam, the gateway to Northeast India, is a land of vibrant culture, lush tea gardens, and majestic wildlife. Known for its rich history and diverse traditions, Assam offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. From the rolling hills of its tea plantations to the dense forests teeming with wildlife, and from the historic temples to the colorful festivals, Assam promises an unforgettable travel experience. Whether you're exploring the famed Kaziranga National Park, cruising along the mighty Brahmaputra River, or soaking in the serenity of its picturesque landscapes, Assam is a destination that captivates and enchants every visitor.

Tour Packages

    4 Days 5 Nights Wildlife Tour

  • Highlights: Kaziranga National Park, Manas National Park, Dibru-Saikhowa National Park.
  • Activities: Jeep safaris, elephant rides, bird watching tours, river cruises.
  • Wildlife: One-horned rhinoceros, Bengal tigers, wild elephants, hoolock gibbons.
  • Accomodations: Jungle lodges, eco-resorts.
  • Experience: Close encounters with diverse wildlife and scenic natural beauty.

    4 Days 5 Nights Cultural Tour

  • Highlights: Kamakhya Temple, Sivasagar, Majuli Island.
  • Activities: Temple tours, cultural performances, interact with local communities, visit Assamese villages.
  • Experience: Discover Assam's rich cultural heritage, traditional festivals, and local crafts.
  • Accomodations: Heritage hotels, comfortable guesthouses.
  • Cuisine: Sample traditional Assamese cuisine and local delicacies.

    4 Days 5 Nights Historical Tour

  • Highlights: Sivasagar, Rang Ghar, Talatal Ghar, Kachari Ruins.
  • Activities: Guided tours to historical sites and monuments, museum visits, exploring ancient ruins.
  • Experience: Learn about Assam's fascinating history and architectural marvels.
  • Accomodations: Heritage hotels, comfortable guesthouses.
  • Insight: Understand the historical significance and legends of Assam.

    4 Days 5 Nights Adventure Tour

  • Highlights: Nameri National Park, Haflong, Dima Hasao.
  • Activities: Trekking, river rafting, rock climbing, camping, mountain biking.
  • Experience: Exciting outdoor adventures amidst Assam's natural landscapes.
  • Accomodations: Adventure camps, eco-lodges.
  • Guides: Experienced adventure guides and instructors.

  • Experience:Peaceful and enriching spiritual experiences in sacred places.
