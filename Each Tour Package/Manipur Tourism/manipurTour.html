Manipur

Manipur, known as the "Jewel of India," offers a rich tapestry of cultural diversity and natural beauty. Nestled in the northeastern part of India, Manipur is renowned for its scenic landscapes, ancient traditions, and vibrant arts. From its serene lakes and majestic hills to its cultural festivals and historical sites, Manipur promises a captivating journey for travelers seeking unique experiences.

Tour Packages

    4 Days 5 Nights Wildlife Tour

  • Highlights: Keibul Lamjao National Park (home of the endangered Sangai deer), Khongjom, Loktak Lake.
  • Activities: Wildlife safaris, bird watching, boat rides, nature walks.
  • Wildlife: Sangai deer, rare bird species, diverse flora
  • Accomodations: Eco-resorts, lakeside cottages.
  • Experience: Explore Manipur's diverse ecosystems and encounter rare wildlife species.

    4 Days 5 Nights Cultural Tour

  • Highlights: Imphal, Moirang, Andro Village.
  • Activities: Visit ancient temples, witness Manipuri dance performances, attend local festivals.
  • Experience: Immerse yourself in Manipur's rich cultural heritage and traditional arts.
  • Accomodations: Homestays, heritage guesthouses.
  • Cuisine: Taste authentic Manipuri cuisine and local delicacies.

    4 Days 5 Nights Historical Tour

  • Highlights: Kangla Fort, War Cemetery, INA Memorial Complex.
  • Activities: Guided tours of historical sites, museum visits, exploration of ancient ruins.
  • Experience: Discover Manipur's historical significance and learn about its role in India's freedom struggle.
  • Accomodations: Comfortable hotels, historic lodges.
  • Insight: Gain insights into Manipur's history and cultural evolution.

    4 Days 5 Nights Adventure Tour

  • Highlights: Dzuko Valley, Shirui Hills, Thalon Cave.
  • Activities: Trekking, camping, rock climbing, rappelling, cave exploration.
  • Experience: Thrilling adventures amidst Manipur's rugged terrain and scenic landscapes.
  • Accomodations: Adventure camps, wilderness lodges.
  • Guides: Experienced trekking guides and adventure specialists.

    4 Days 5 Nights Spiritual Tour

  • Highlights: Shri Govindjee Temple, Shree Shree Govindajee Temple, Kaina Temple.
  • Activities: Temple visits, meditation retreats, spiritual discourses, nature walks.
  • Experience: Peaceful and spiritual experiences in Manipur's tranquil settings.
  • Accomodations: Monastery stays, spiritual retreat centers.
  • Atmosphere: Serene surroundings ideal for introspection and relaxation.

    4 Days 5 Nights Scenic Tour

  • Highlights: Loktak Lake, Khonghampat Orchidarium, Thalon Cave.
  • Activities: Nature photography, boating, picnics, fishing, leisurely walks.
  • Experience: Relaxation amidst Manipur's natural beauty and serene environments.
  • Accomodations: Boutique resorts, scenic lodges.
  • Cuisine: Enjoy regional cuisine in picturesque settings.
