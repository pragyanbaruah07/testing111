Meghalaya

Meghalaya, aptly named the "Abode of Clouds," is a state of breathtaking natural beauty and vibrant cultural heritage. Famous for its living root bridges, rolling hills, and lush greenery, Meghalaya offers a serene escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The state is home to some of the wettest places on Earth, stunning waterfalls, and unique biodiversity. From exploring the mystical caves and majestic waterfalls to experiencing the rich traditions of its indigenous tribes, Meghalaya promises a memorable and enchanting journey. Whether you're trekking through the verdant forests, enjoying the picturesque landscapes, or participating in lively local festivals, Meghalaya is a destination that will captivate your heart.

Tour Packages

    4 Days 5 Nights Wildlife Tour

  • Highlights: Nokrek National Park, Balpakram National Park, Siju Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Activities: Wildlife safaris, bird watching tours, nature walks, cave explorations.
  • Wildlife: Red pandas, elephants, hoolock gibbons, rare orchids.
  • Accomodations: Jungle lodges, eco-resorts.
  • Experience: Encounter unique wildlife and explore Meghalaya’s rich biodiversity.

    4 Days 5 Nights Cultural Tour

  • Highlights: Shillong, Cherrapunjee, Khasi Villages.
  • Activities: Visit traditional villages, witness tribal dances, participate in local festivals, explore local markets.
  • Experience: Immerse yourself in the rich traditions and vibrant culture of the Khasi, Jaintia, and Garo tribes.
  • Accomodations: Homestays, heritage hotels.
  • Cuisine: Taste traditional Meghalayan cuisine and local delicacies.

    4 Days 5 Nights Historical Tour

  • Highlights: Monoliths of Nartiang, Nongkrem, Mawphlang Sacred Forest.
  • Activities: Guided tours to historical sites, exploration of ancient monoliths, visit sacred groves.
  • Experience: Discover the historical and spiritual significance of Meghalaya’s ancient sites.
  • Accomodations: Heritage hotels, comfortable guesthouses.
  • Insight: Learn about the history and legends of Meghalaya.

    4 Days 5 Nights Adventure Tour

  • Highlights: Living Root Bridges, David Scott Trail, Laitlum Canyons.
  • Activities: Trekking, caving, rock climbing, camping, river canyoning.
  • Experience: Thrilling adventures in the rugged terrains and lush landscapes of Meghalaya.
  • Accomodations: Adventure camps, eco-lodges.
  • Guides: Experienced adventure guides and instructors.
